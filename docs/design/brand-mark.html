The idea

The final mark fuses the two directions we liked. From Spike it takes the volleyball — the curved panel seams that make it unmistakably a ball. From Rally it takes the gold trend line — data, momentum, a performance climbing upward. Together they say exactly what VolleyVision is: a serious stats tool that belongs courtside.

What it means
Volleyball seams — precise
The ring and three curved panel seams read instantly as a volleyball. Clean, exact, no clutter.
Gold trend line — motivating
A bold stat line climbs across the ball — every touch tracked, performance rising. Heavier than the seams so the data always stands out.
Breakout arrow — direct
The line breaks out of the ball into an upward arrow — forward motion, the elite trajectory, no ornament.
